Nightclub

Leipätehdas is a unique nightclub with an good army of house DJ:s. We concentrate on playing the music you want to hear. Live music is also a big part of Leipätehdas, ranging from bigger to smaller artists. Artists like: Rotten Sound, 1g3b, Haloo Helsinki, Uniikki, Paul Di Anno, Mangoo, Timshel, Gilby Clarke, Stig, The Irrationals etc.

Terrace

Leipätehdas offers a big terrace with evening sun. It's open summertime, every day (with weather reservation) 17-.

D.O.M. Munkhaus

D.O.M. is like a second living room, with board games, xbox 360 and a cozy atmosphere. It is a pub with a wide selection of beers and alot of variety. Every tuesday we have an pubquiz on D.O.M. starting at 21:00. (Exept summertime).

Karaoke

In Leipätehdas there is also a karaokebar. But it is even more than just that. Here you can also find board games, beer pong tables and an playlist containing english, finnish and swedish hits and classics.
