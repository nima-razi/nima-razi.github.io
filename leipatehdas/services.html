Nightclub

A unique nightclub with an army of house DJ:s concentrating on playing music you want to hear. Live music's a big part of Leipätehdas, ranging from all sorts of artists like: Rotten Sound, 1g3b, Haloo Helsinki, Uniikki, Paul Di Anno, Mangoo, Timshel, Gilby Clarke, Stig, The Irrationals etc.

Terrace

Leipätehdas offers a big terrace with evening sun. It's open summertime, every day (with weather reservation) 17-.

D.O.M. Munkhaus

Like a second living room with a cozy atmosphere. It's a pub with a variety of bewerages and games. Pubquiz held every tuesday starting at 21:00.

Karaoke

There's also a karaokebar and it's even more than just that. Here you can also find board games, beer pong tables and an playlist containing english, finnish and swedish hits and classics.
